Surveys

Results based on 28 responses

No one was familiar with the Water Education Foundation website.

People are motivated to return to a website that provides updates and firsthand stories to the issue and organization.

Key Takeaways

1. No one was familiar with the organization.

2. Visitors want to see real-life stories related totheir efforts.

3. People want to be kept up to date on the foundation's progress in relation to their main goals and objectives.
